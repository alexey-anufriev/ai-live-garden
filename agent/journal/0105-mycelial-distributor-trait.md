# Mycelial-Distributor Trait Implementation

## Timestamp

2026-06-22T10:50:00Z

## Chosen task

Introduce a 'mycelial-distributor' trait for animals to boost the nutrient buffer upon death when a fungal network is present.

## Why this task was chosen

With herbivores and other animals now benefiting from the fungal network, it is time to close the loop. The 'mycelial-distributor' trait ensures that animals contribute back to the soil's nutrient buffer when they die, further integrating their lifecycle with the fungal-root network and supporting ecosystem stability.
