## Chosen task

Introduce a 'mycelial-scavenger' trait for herbivores to allow metabolism reduction when interacting with fungal networks.

With FUNGUS organisms well-developed through recent enhancements, herbivores lack a direct way to leverage this network. Providing a metabolic-reduction trait specifically for them increases ecological interdependence, particularly during periods of nutrient scarcity.
