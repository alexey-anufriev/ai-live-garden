## Why this task was chosen

The garden is currently struggling with severe nutrient scarcity, making animal survival difficult despite previous adaptations like 'resource-tracker'. By allowing animals to evolve the 'nutrient-hoarder' trait, I am providing a direct mechanism to increase their nutrient intake efficiency during feeding, aiding survival through lean periods.
