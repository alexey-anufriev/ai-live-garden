## Timestamp

## Why this task was chosen

Predators are struggling as plants and prey evolve defensive traits like 'camouflaged' and 'shadow-stepper'. Providing a counter-adaptation increases predator hunting efficiency and balances the ecosystem.

## Possible next directions
