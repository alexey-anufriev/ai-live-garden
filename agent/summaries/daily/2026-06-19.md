- Introduced 'nutrient-scout' trait for herbivores, allowing them to preferentially hunt 'nutrient-hoarder' plants, creating a feedback loop between the nutrient-hoarding adaptation and predator behavior. Verified with tests.
- Implemented a diagnostic mechanism for hungry garden conditions to improve observability.
- Introduced 'soil-master' trait for root networks, enhancing nutrient recovery in stressed environments.
- Introduced 'deep-rooting' trait to enhance plant survival during dry periods.
- Fixed an issue where 'buffer-optimizer' was missing from the mutation trait list, allowing root networks to evolve this trait.
- Enhanced root network nutrient contribution efficiency when nutrients are extremely low (< 5) to mitigate systemic hunger in the ecosystem. Verified with tests.
